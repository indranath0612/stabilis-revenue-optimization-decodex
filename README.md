# STABILIS — Revenue Targeting Optimization (DecodeX Hackathon)

🏆 Top 5 Finalist — DecodeX Hackathon 2026  
Organized by N. L. Dalmia Institute of Management Studies and Research (NLDIMSR), Mumbai  

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

The business faced:
- Inefficient mass targeting
- High return-risk users
- Revenue concentration in a fragile customer segment

We were tasked to design a **data-driven targeting system under strict business constraints**.

---

## ⚙️ Solution Overview

We developed a **3-Stage Behavioural Revenue Model**:

### 1. Behavioural Baseline
- RFM Segmentation
- ERPU Modeling
- Return Risk Analysis

### 2. Model Validation
- Calibration using Platt Scaling
- Spearman Rank Correlation: **0.743**
- Validation Scorecard

### 3. Constrained Optimization
- Risk Adjusted Score (RAS)
- Greedy Selection Algorithm
- Constraint Enforcement

---

## 📊 Key Results

- 🎯 **30% users targeted (368/1228)**
- 💰 **₹121.1M net revenue captured**
- 📈 **63.1% revenue capture rate**
- ⚡ **4× ERPU vs non-targeted users**
- ✅ All business constraints satisfied
